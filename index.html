What is NAYS

"You may be able to move forward quickly by yourself, but you only can go far while accompanied by others."At the North American Youth Summit, we come together to learn how to "shed some weight": going from our hometowns to foreign lands, from schools entering into society, we unknowingly accumulated a lot of baggage. We want to cast off all these excess burdens together.

Who we are

The Dao Lab is an innovative club that brings together a group of self-motivated young people. We work together to support more entrepreneurs, family business successors, and overseas students in exploring Chinese classics and Western management theories. Through practical experience and innovation, we help them discover their life missions, enhance their corporate philosophy, and implement entrepreneurship and innovation. Our goal is to create a new frontier for personal growth and business success through a collaborative learning and creation mechanism, guided by the values of "self-cultivation, family harmony, and global prosperity".
